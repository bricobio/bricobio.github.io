About

Hi!

We're Bricobio: Montr√©al's Do-it-yourself/together-bio community. We are an organization dedicated to science research, education and communication. In our community, we embrace the maker/hacker movement and values, and apply them in the context of biotechnology. That means lots of accessibility, sharing, openness, freedom and passion.

We're a young group at a little over 1 year old. And, we've spent most of this past year running workshops, planning and meeting with others. We're just about ready to establish ourselves as a non-profit organization and rent a lab.

At the moment, we're focusing on finding funding, space, equipment as well as defining our core values and structure (which includes writing this "About" page).

If you're a normal/abnormal person with an interest in biotech, an aspiring scientist, or a tenured professor, you're welcome to join! We'd like to invite everyone to visit us at our next event or in our lab, once we get setup :) So, come join us, hang out, learn and enjoy the ride!
