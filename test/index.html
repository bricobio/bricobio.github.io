About us

Bricobio is the community biology lab of Montreal, Canada. Like a maker or hacker space, our main goal is to make tools and knowledge for doing biology available and accessible to a wider community and general public. Our core values include:

  • Openness
  • Accessibility
  • Transparency
  • Community-focus

We are also sometimes known as biohackers and part of the Do-it-yourself Biology movement.
