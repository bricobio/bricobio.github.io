We're running another hackathon!

Hackerfest 2017

For the second consecutive year, we're hosting a mini-outdoor-hackathon at Hackerfest!

What happens at Hackerfest

In advance, we plan our projects and gather materials. Then at the event we arrive Friday evening and start building! There will be a few talks from special guests, and aside from that we are hanging out under the stars and building crazy biology stuff.

Check the Google Doc, and check back here for updates!

This year's theme is: GROW!

We're focused on building tools and things for growing stuff! That means we're building things like: growth chambers, fermentation tanks, temperature sensors, and anything that will be handy for growing organisms!

...and by organisms, we mean algae, yeast, bacteria for food and fun.

Like other maker or hacker spaces, our main goal is to make tools and knowledge for doing biology available and accessible to a wider community and general public. Our core values include:

  • Openness
  • Accessibility
  • Transparency
  • Community-focus

We are also sometimes known as biohackers and part of the Do-it-yourself Biology movement.

Come hang out!

Comme les autres makerspace / fablabs / hackerspaces, notre bût primaire est de créer un espace et un communauté ouverts pour que le publique peut apprendre, faire des expériences et dévlopper des projets en biologie. Nos valeurs incluent:

  • Ouverture
  • Acces
  • Transparence
  • Communauté

Parfois, nous sommes aussi connus comme des "biohackers" et partie du mouvement de biologie faites-vous-même.
