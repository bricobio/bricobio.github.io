Makerspace / Fab Lab / Bio Lab

Bricobio is a shared workshop/lab for doing biology. To access the space you can join our community by becoming a member or volunteer!

At our space we have...

  • Micro and molecular biology tools
  • Aquariums, incubators, fridge and freezer storage
  • sterile area (coming soon!)
  • 3D printing, electronics and other maker tools via Helios Makerspace

which you can use to...

  • Experiment with DNA testing and editing
  • Grow food, fungi, fabric, algae, or other materials
  • Learn about synthetic biology, cellular agriculture and other future technologies

Volunteers provide safety training to all members.

How to join the community

Join an open night!

Find out by subscribing to our mailing list! Usually we meet on Wednesday or Thursday nights.

Become a member or volunteer

  • Members get access to the space with supervision from a volunteer or during open hours (TBD).
  • Members and volunteers can work on their own projects and use the equipment in the space.
  • Volunteers help build/maintain the space and get free access!
  • The weekly commitment for volunteers is 8 hours.
  • Some experience is necessary.

More about us

Like other maker or hacker spaces, our main goal is to make tools and knowledge for doing biology available and accessible to a wider community and general public. Our core values include:

  • Openness
  • Accessibility
  • Transparency
  • Community-focus
  • Safety

We are also sometimes known as biohackers, Do-it-yourself Biology (DIYBio) or community biologists.

We're also always looking for donations of equipment or materials that we can use in our labs. Please reach out if you can help!

En plus à propos

Comme les autres makerspace / fablabs / hackerspaces, notre bût primaire est de créer un espace et un communauté ouverts pour que le publique peut apprendre, faire des expériences et dévlopper des projets en biologie. Nos valeurs incluent:

  • Ouverture
  • Acces
  • Transparence
  • Communauté
  • Sécurité

Parfois, nous sommes aussi connus comme des "biohackers" et partie du mouvement de biologie faites-vous-même ou biologie communautaire.

Nous somme souvent en cherchant des dons des équipments et matières pour notre laboratoire. SVP contactez-nous si vous pouvez nous aider!
